Detecting Fraud on the Secondary Event Market

The Model

Our final model utilizes a Random Forest to predict on the numerical features. Due to the fradulent class only representing ~10% of the data, we employed the method of undersampling to help balance this. The model provides us with an F1 score (the harmonic mean between percision and recall) of .89. While we conducted extensive work into the realm of natural language processing (F1 score of .92!), we were unable to utilize this feature in our model when launching this app. Look for this exciting addition in a future version!

Problem

As more and more people look to the secondary market for event tickets, preventing fraudulent activity becomes increasingly important to protect both the user, as well as the reputation of the marketplace. We are tasked with reliably predicting fraud, with an end-goal of preventing fraudulent transactions before they can take place.
